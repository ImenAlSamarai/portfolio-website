01. Background

I began my career in Astroparticle Physics, working with international teams at CERN and LPNHE. My research focused on extracting weak signals from high-dimensional, noisy datasets using advanced statistical and machine learning techniques — resulting in over 100 publications, including a high-impact discovery in Science. I also built large-scale simulation and data processing systems adopted across multiple experiments.

In 2018, I moved into quantitative finance, applying the same analytical rigor to model market behavior, risk, and returns. As Lead Data Scientist at Swiss Life and later Quantitative Research Director at TD Securities, I led teams building AI systems for systematic trading, NLP-driven market intelligence, and portfolio optimization — delivering measurable alpha and real-time trading insights.

Today, I'm focused on building AI-powered products that solve real-world problems. I bring a blend of deep technical expertise, strategic insight, and hands-on experience deploying machine learning in high-stakes environments. I also advise organizations on AI and data strategy — turning complexity into clarity and impact.
